Turkey Lavash Wraps

This wrap is hearty enough to keep you full until dinner, but not so heavy you require a post-lunch nap. Filled with a zingy arugula-dill salad, sliced turkey, roasted red peppers, and crunchy cucumbers, and sealed with a creamy goat cheese spread, it's exactly the kind of colorful lunch we crave to brighten up our weekdays.

Ingredients

  • Scallion
  • Crumbled goat cheese
  • Plain whole-milk Greek yogurt
  • Extra-virgin olive oil
  • Kosher salt
  • Black pepper
  • Arugula
  • Coarsley chopped fresh dill
  • Fresh lemon juice
  • Store-bought or homemade lavash
  • Sliced deli turkey
  • Roasted red peppers
  • Persin cucumbers

Kitchen Supplies

  • Medium mixing bowl
  • Large bowl
  • Measuring cups
  • Tablespoons

Recipe

Step 1: In a medium bowl, combine scallion, goat cheese, yogurt, and 2 teaspoons oil; season with salt and pepper.


Step 2: In a large bowl, gently toss arugula, dill, lemon juice, and remaining 1 teaspoon oil to combine.


Step 3: On a work surface, lay out lavash. Spread goat cheese mixture in an even layer over bread, leaving a 1" border on all sides. Divide turkey, red peppers, cucumbers, and arugula salad among wraps.


Step 3: Pull one half of lavash up and over filling like a burrito, then tightly wrap to enclose. Tuck in ends to seal. Cut in half before serving.
