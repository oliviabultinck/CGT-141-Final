About Olivia Bultinck

Hello, my name is Olivia Bultinck and I am a freshman attending Purdue University double majoring in Animation and Visual Effects and Game Design and Development. I am from a town in Northwest Indiana near Notre Dame University. Art has been a big part of my life ever since I was a little kid drawing with sidewalk chalk for my neighbors to see. As I grew up I continued with my passion of art and it has stuck with me since. With that being said, at Purdue I hope to gain knowledge and experience to help me land a job in the industry upon graduation doing what I love and enjoy.
