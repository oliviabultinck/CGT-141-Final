Buffalo Chicken Grilled Cheese

There’s no shame in a hearty dip for dinner in our book, but if you need a good excuse to enjoy your favorite warm dip without any shame, then let us introduce Buffalo chicken’s new best friend: grilled cheese. This combination is not only ridiculously good, but it’s so easy to make, you’ll wonder why you haven’t been turning your favorite dip into sandwich form all this time. Whether you’re looking for a game day dinner or a post-Super Bowl snack to use up your leftovers, this grilled cheese is a game-changer.

Ingredients

  • Rotisserie chicken
  • Cream cheese
  • Buffalo sauce
  • Shredded sharp white cheedar (or Monterey Jack)
  • Butter
  • Bread

Kitchen Supplies

  • Sauté pan
  • Large mixing bowls
  • Tablespoons
  • Measuring cups

Recipe

Step 1: In a large mixing bowl, combine chicken, cream cheese, buffalo sauce, and white cheddar. Slather on bread and form sandwiches, then set aside.


Step 2: In a sauté pan over medium heat, melt butter. Add sandwiches and cook until golden brown, about 2 minutes. Flip and grill the other side, 1 to 2 minutes.
